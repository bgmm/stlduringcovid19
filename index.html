Rediscovering the architecture of St. Louis, MO

The Architecture of St. Louis

The book named "A Guide to the Architecture of St. Louis" by George McCue and Frank Peters has been standing on our shelf for a while. During COVID-19, finally, we have time to rediscover St. Louis with the guide of this book. And we wanted to document and share our experience while enjoying the different parts of the city.
